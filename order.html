Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by selecting "Order Online," choosing delivery or pickup, and selecting your desired items. You can also call us directly at (123) 456-7890 to place an order over the phone.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay. For online orders, payment is processed securely through our website.

Do you offer vegetarian or gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Look for the dietary icons next to menu items to identify suitable choices. Our staff is also happy to accommodate dietary requirementsâ€”just let us know!

How long will my delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes, depending on your location and current order volume. You'll receive an estimated delivery time when you place your order, and you can always call us for updates.

Can I modify my order after it's placed?

We'll do our best to accommodate changes to your order, but it depends on when you contact us. If your order is already being prepared, modifications may not be possible. Please call us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.
