About Shawarma Queen

Our story, our passion, and our commitment to authentic Mediterranean cuisine.

Our Story

Shawarma Queen was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring authentic, flavorful Mediterranean cuisine to our community. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our commitment to quality and tradition.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing the true flavors of the Middle East to your table. Every dish is prepared with care, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

The secret to our success? Our vertical rotisseries, where we slow-cook our specially marinated meats to juicy perfection. Our signature shawarma, with its perfect balance of spices and tender texture, has become a customer favorite and the cornerstone of our menu.

At Shawarma Queen, we believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's an experience that brings people together. Our restaurant is designed to be a warm, welcoming space where friends and families can gather to enjoy delicious food and create lasting memories.

We take pride in offering a diverse menu that caters to various dietary preferences, including vegetarian, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Everyone deserves to enjoy the rich flavors of Mediterranean cuisine, regardless of dietary restrictions.

As we continue to grow, our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction remains unwavering. We're grateful for the support of our loyal customers and look forward to serving you for many years to come.
